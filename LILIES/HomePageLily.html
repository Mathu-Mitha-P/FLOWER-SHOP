LILY COLLECTION A lily is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the Lilium genus in the Liliaceae family. Lilies are known for their large, showy flowers, which typically have six petal-like segments and come in various colors, such as white, yellow, pink, orange, and red. They are often grown as ornamental plants in gardens and are also popular in floral arrangements. Lilies are known for their sweet fragrance and can symbolize purity, refined beauty, and transience in different cultures.
