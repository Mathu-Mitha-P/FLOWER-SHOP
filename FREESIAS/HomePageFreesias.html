FREESIAS COLLECTION Freesias are fragrant, tube-shaped flowers that belong to the genus Freesia, which is native to South Africa. They are popular for their sweet, delicate scent and are often used in bouquets and floral arrangements. Freesias come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, pink, red, purple, and blue. They symbolize innocence, friendship, and thoughtfulness. In the language of flowers, freesias are associated with purity, trust, and good fortune, making them a meaningful choice for weddings, celebrations, or as a gift to show appreciation and affection.
