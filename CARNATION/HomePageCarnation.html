CARNATIONS COLLECTION Carnations (Dianthus caryophyllus) are flowering plants known for their frilled, ruffled petals and sweet, clove-like fragrance. They are native to the Mediterranean region and are popular in floral arrangements due to their wide range of colors, including white, pink, red, yellow, and purple. Carnations are symbols of love, fascination, and distinction, with different colors carrying specific meanings.
