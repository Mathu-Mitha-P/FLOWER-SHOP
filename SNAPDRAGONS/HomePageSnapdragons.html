SNAPDRAGONS COLLECTION A snapdragon is a flowering plant belonging to the genus Antirrhinum, which is native to the Mediterranean region and North America. Known for their distinctive, tubular flowers that resemble a dragon's mouth (hence the name), snapdragons come in a variety of colors including red, pink, yellow, white, and purple. The flowers can be single or clustered on tall spikes.Snapdragons are popular in gardens and floral arrangements due to their unique appearance and wide range of colors. In the language of flowers, snapdragons symbolize strength, grace, and resilience. They are also associated with feelings of ambition, charm, and protection.
