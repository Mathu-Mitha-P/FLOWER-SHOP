CHRYSANTHEMUM COLLECTION A chrysanthemum is a flowering plant of the genus Chrysanthemum, native to Asia and northeastern Europe. It is widely cultivated for its bright and colorful flowers, which are often associated with autumn and are popular in gardens, bouquets, and floral arrangements. Chrysanthemums are also culturally significant in many countries, particularly in Japan and China, where they symbolize longevity, honor, and happiness.
