HYDRANGEAS COLLECTION Hydrangeas are flowering plants belonging to the genus Hydrangea, native to Asia and the Americas. They are known for their large, showy clusters of flowers that come in a range of colors, including blue, pink, white, and purple. The color of hydrangea flowers can change based on the pH level of the soil; for example, blue flowers typically grow in acidic soil, while pink flowers thrive in alkaline soil. Hydrangeas are often associated with grace, beauty, and abundance. In the language of flowers, they can symbolize heartfelt emotions, understanding, and the promise of a lasting relationship.
