LISIANTHUS COLLECTION Lisianthus (Eustoma grandiflorum) is a flowering plant native to warm regions of the United States, Mexico, the Caribbean, and South America. It is known for its delicate, rose-like blooms in a variety of colors, including white, pink, purple, and blue. Lisianthus flowers are often associated with appreciation, gratitude, and charisma, making them popular in bouquets and floral arrangements. Their soft, elegant appearance makes them a favorite for weddings and other special occasions.
